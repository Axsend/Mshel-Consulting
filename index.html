10 Years Experience

Dr Barka Umaru Mshelia is the founder and chief executive officer of Mshel homes, a leading real estate company in Nigeria.

He is credited with introducing contemporary designs and smart homes in Abuja in locations such as Maitama, Asokoro, Guzape, Katampe extensions, Kyami , Aviation village, Lugbe and Idu etc.

He has also built a discipline in business strategy and innovation at the highest levels locally and internationally. He has consulted in real estate development , business development, strategy, innovation and growth, he is a mentor and a life coach who has passion to see Jesus Christ glorified in peoples lives.
